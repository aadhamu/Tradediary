Trading Plan

Account Size: $5k

Some Trading Rules

  1. Don't breach daily drawdown.
  2. Don't breach overall account drawdown.
  3. Always use stop-loss orders to limit potential losses on each trade.
  4. Determine the appropriate amount of capital to risk on each trade based on your overall portfolio and risk tolerance.
  5. Don't trade excessively. Stick to your trading plan and only enter trades that meet your criteria.
  6. Stay calm and avoid making impulsive decisions based on emotions.

My Personal Rules

  1. Every trade should be part of a well-defined strategy with clear entry, exit, and risk management rules.
  2. Understand that losses are part of trading.
  3. Avoid making impulsive trades or deviating from your established trading strategy based on short-term market movements or emotions.
  4. Record every trade, including the rationale, outcome, and any lessons learned. Review your journal regularly to identify patterns and improve your strategy.
  5. Establish achievable trading goals and benchmarks. Aim for steady progress rather than unrealistic returns.
